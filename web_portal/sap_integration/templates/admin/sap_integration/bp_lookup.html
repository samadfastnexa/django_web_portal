SAP Business Partner Lookup

Look up a single SAP Business Partner (customer, vendor or lead) directly from the SAP Service Layer by its CardCode. Use this when you need to:

  • Verify whether a CardCode exists in SAP before linking it to a dealer.
  • Inspect a partner's master-data attributes (group, VAT, balances) without opening SAP B1.
  • Confirm the integration is reachable and returning the expected JSON shape.

Calls the SAP company database currently selected in your admin session. No data is written or modified.
